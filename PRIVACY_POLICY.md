# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions in this repository.

## Data Collection

These extensions do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No information about you or your browsing activity is sent to any server or third party.

## Permissions

Each extension requests only the permissions necessary for its functionality. No extension accesses data outside of its declared scope.

### textless-x-extension

- Runs only on `https://x.com/*`
- Modifies the visual appearance of the page by hiding certain elements
- Does not read, store, or transmit any page content or user data

## Local Storage

These extensions do not use cookies, local storage, or any other form of persistent storage.

## Third Parties

These extensions do not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Changes

If this policy is updated, the date at the top of this file will reflect the most recent revision.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue in this repository.
